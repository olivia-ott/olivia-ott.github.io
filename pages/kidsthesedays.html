Kids These Days

“Kids These Days" is a visual analysis on how different people of different ages experienced their childhood, starting from the 50's to the early 2000's. Using information from research and surveys, I created 6 decade-based posters that compare topics in respect to childhood such as movies and tv shows, toys and games, technology, music, significant cultural events, and more. The colors and patterns used in each poster are reflective of the time period. I’ve also included 12 extra individual illustrations as stickers.
