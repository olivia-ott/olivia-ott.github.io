ABOUT

Olivia Ott is a graphic designer from New Orleans, Louisiana and a graduate of Louisiana State University. She is a versatile designer with experience in digital advertising, social media, logos and branding, web design, print, and more. Her process is rooted in research, communication, and visual problem solving; she strives to create effective, compelling solutions through her work.
