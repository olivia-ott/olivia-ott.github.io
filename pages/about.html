ABOUT

Olivia Ott is a graphic designer from New Orleans, Louisiana. She currently attends Louisiana State University in Baton Rouge where she will graduate in May 2020. She is a versatile designer with experience in web design, logo design, branding, social media, publication, and more. Her process is rooted in research, communication, and visual problem solving; she strives to create effective, compelling solutions through her work.
