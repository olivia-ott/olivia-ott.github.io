Around the World in 26 Letters

Around the World in 26 Letters is a book and postcard collection created to encourage travel and exploration among a wide audience. The book contains illustrations and descriptions for 26 different cities from various countries, with one city for each letter of the alphabet. The project includes postcards for each individual city as well. The alphabetical aspect and engaging illustrations cater to younger audiences, while the informative descriptions provide geographical and cultural insight for more mature audiences.
