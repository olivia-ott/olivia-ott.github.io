Sorellas Boutique

Sorellas is a quaint boutique located in Old Metairie, Louisiana. The shop’s style is based on vintage finds and elegant patterns, and the owners wanted their logo and branding to reflect that. The golden sunburst combined with the deep purples and pinks in the text reflect both art deco styles and feminine fashion trends. The complementing pattern consists of simply illustrated fashion related icons to support the brand’s detailed logo.
