Chase Homes

Chase Homes, LLC is a construction company based in Metairie, Louisiana. Looking to upgrade their branding, they were in search of a modern style that reflected the residential construction industry. The logo consists of a “C” and “H” built together with clean lines. The overall shape of the logo resembles a house, and the vibrant yellow accent color was chosen to reflect the builders’ hard hat.
